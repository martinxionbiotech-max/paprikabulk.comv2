**Description:** Special Grade (ASTA200+) is the highest color-intensity paprika powder produced by Dinweys. Sourced exclusively from high-capsanthin *Capsicum annuum* cultivars grown in Xinjiang's high-altitude, arid climate — which naturally concentrates pigment development. With a minimum ASTA of 200 and exceptionally low SHU (≤500), this grade is the premier choice for oleoresin extraction (highest oleoresin yield per kg) and applications requiring maximum red color intensity. Particle size is customizable up to 100 mesh.

## Processing Recommendations

### Oleoresin Extraction (Primary Application)
- **Recommended solvent:** Hexane or ethanol (food-grade, per customer process)
- **Expected oleoresin yield:** 8–12% w/w with ASTA 1,000+ in finished oleoresin
- **Grind optimization:** 60–80 mesh recommended for extraction — finer grind increases surface area, improving extraction efficiency by 5–10%
- **Extraction temperature:** 40–60°C; avoid >70°C to prevent pigment degradation

### Powder Blending for Color Enhancement
When used as a color booster in blended paprika powder:

| Target Blend ASTA | Special Grade % | Base Grade % |
|-------------------|-----------------|--------------|
| 180 | 20% Special + 80% Premium (160 base) | — |
| 160 | 50% Special + 50% Superior (120 base) | — |
| 140 | 30% Special + 70% Standard (90 base) | — |
| 200 | 100% Special | — |

### Dosing (Direct Application)
- **Snack coatings:** 2–5% w/w of seasoning blend (color impact equivalent to 6–12% Premium powder)
- **Dry rubs:** 10–25% w/w
- **Color-sensitive consumer products:** 5–15% w/w of total spice mix

### Milling Options
Special Grade is available from standard 40 mesh up to 100 mesh. Note: finer grind reduces bulk density and increases production cost.

### Special Shipping Considerations
Given the high pigment concentration, Special Grade is more susceptible to color loss from heat and UV than lower grades. Reefer container (18–22°C, dark) is strongly recommended for all shipments exceeding 20 days transit or with destinations in warm climates.

## Storage Stability Data

| Condition | Shelf Life | Expected Outcome |
|-----------|------------|------------------|
| 15–20°C, ≤55% RH, dark, sealed | 18–24 months | ASTA loss <10% |
| 20–25°C, ≤60% RH, dark | 12–15 months | ASTA loss 10–15% |
| 25–30°C, ≤60% RH, dark | 9–12 months | ASTA loss 15–20% |
| >30°C or >65% RH | Not recommended | Rapid degradation; >30% ASTA loss in 6 months |
| Vacuum-sealed (foil) | 24 months at ≤25°C | Best option for long-term storage |
| After opening | 2–4 months | Re-seal in airtight container |

**Critical:** Special Grade has more pigment surface area and is therefore **more sensitive** to ambient conditions than lower grades. Strict temperature control and complete UV exclusion are non-negotiable.
