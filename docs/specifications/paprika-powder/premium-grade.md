| HS Code | 0904.22.00 (crushed/ground Capsicum) |
| Country of Origin | China (Xinjiang / Shandong), optionally Spain or Peru |

**Description:** Premium Grade paprika powder is produced exclusively from fully ripened, deep-red *Capsicum annuum* L. pods. The pods are selected for uniform color development, deseeded, dried under controlled conditions, and milled to a fine (40 mesh) powder. This grade delivers a rich red hue (ASTA 160–200) with a mild, sweet flavor profile ideal for top-tier food manufacturing, retail seasoning blends, and applications where visual appeal and consistent color are critical. No anti-caking agents, flow aids, or artificial colorants are added.

## Processing Recommendations

### Milling & Sieving
Premium Grade is milled through 40 mesh screens. For finer grinds (60–80 mesh), custom toll-milling can be arranged — note that increasing mesh may reduce bulk density and slightly affect flowability.

### Blending Guidelines
- **Blending with other spices:** Premium Grade blends uniformly with granulated garlic, onion powder, black pepper, and oregano. Use a ribbon or V-blender; recommend 10–15 minutes at 20–30 RPM.
- **Blending with salt/sugar:** Add paprika powder last to avoid moisture absorption from hydroscopic ingredients.
- **Color enhancement:** Blending with a small percentage (5–10%) of Special Grade (ASTA 200+) paprika powder can push the blend's color value to ASTA 180+.

### Dosing Recommendations
- **Sauces & soups:** 0.5–2.0% w/w of total formulation for desired red hue.
- **Snack coatings:** 3–8% w/w of seasoning blend.
- **Dry rubs:** 15–40% w/w of rub formulation.

### Stability Consideration
Paprika powder is heat- and light-sensitive. For processes exceeding 120°C surface temperature, consider post-cooking application or use of encapsulated paprika oleoresin for color stability.

## Storage Stability Data

| Condition | Shelf Life | Expected Outcome |
|-----------|------------|------------------|
| 15–20°C, ≤55% RH, dark | 18 months | ASTA loss <10% from initial value |
| 20–25°C, ≤60% RH, dark | 12 months | ASTA loss 10–15% from initial value |
| 25–30°C, ≤60% RH, dark | 9 months | ASTA loss 15–25% from initial value |
| >30°C or >65% RH | Not recommended | Accelerated color degradation, caking risk |
| Sealed, unopened | 12–18 months | Depending on storage temperature (see above) |
| After opening | 3–6 months | Reseal immediately; store in cool, dark cabinet |

**Critical Storage Requirements:**
- Store in a cool, dry, dark environment; temperature fluctuations ≥10°C accelerate moisture migration and caking.
- Protect from direct sunlight and UV exposure — ASTA color degrades rapidly under UV.
- Keep away from ammonia, cleaning chemicals, and strong-smelling ingredients (paprika readily absorbs volatile odors).
- **Do not refrigerate** — condensation risk upon removal causes caking and mold growth.
- Relative humidity must remain ≤60% at all times; moisture pickup above 12% w/w triggers mold risk.
