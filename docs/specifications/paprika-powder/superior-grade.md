**Description:** Superior Grade paprika powder is produced from fully ripened red *Capsicum annuum* L. pods selected for balanced color (ASTA 120–160) and mild heat profile (≤1,000 SHU). It undergoes controlled drying, deseeded milling, and 40 mesh sieving. This grade represents the optimal price-to-performance ratio for bulk food manufacturing where good red color is required without the cost premium of Premium-grade material. No anti-caking agents or artificial additives are used.

## Processing Recommendations

### Milling & Sieving
Standard 40 mesh. For specialty orders, 20 mesh (coarser) or 60 mesh (finer) available — consult your account manager for lead time and pricing.

### Blending Guidelines
- **Color optimization:** If the final application needs ASTA >150, blend Superior Grade (75–85%) with Premium (15–25%) to lift the overall color value.
- **Heat tolerance:** For processes >100°C, consider blending with oleoresin instead of powder for better stability and color retention.
- **Anti-caking:** If blending with very hygroscopic ingredients (sugar, salt, onion powder), add 0.5–1.0% silicon dioxide (E551) as a flow aid — verify regulatory compliance for target market.

### Dosing Recommendations
- **Sauces:** 0.5–1.5% w/w of total formulation
- **Processed meats:** 0.2–0.5% w/w
- **Seasoning blends:** 10–30% w/w

## Storage Stability Data

| Condition | Shelf Life | Expected Outcome |
|-----------|------------|------------------|
| 15–20°C, ≤55% RH, dark | 15–18 months | ASTA loss <10% |
| 20–25°C, ≤60% RH, dark | 12 months | ASTA loss 10–15% |
| 25–30°C, ≤60% RH, dark | 9 months | ASTA loss 15–25% |
| >30°C or >65% RH | Not recommended | Degradation, caking |
| Sealed, unopened | 12–15 months | Per condition above |
| After opening | 3–5 months | Reseal; store cool & dark |

**Critical:**
- Keep ≤60% RH — moisture absorption above 12% triggers mold.
- Protect from UV light — ASTA degrades rapidly under direct sunlight.
- Avoid temperature fluctuations >10°C — causes condensation within packaging.
- Do not store near volatile chemicals (cleaning agents, ammonia).
