**Description:** Special Grade ASTA200+ paprika flakes are the highest color-intensity flake product offered by Dinweys. Sourced exclusively from Xinjiang high-capsanthin *Capsicum annuum* cultivars, these flakes deliver minimum ASTA 200 color value with low SHU (≤500). Each flake retains deep red color that commands premium retail pricing. The coarse 4–8 mesh cut maintains piece identity, making them a superior garnish and decorative ingredient for high-end retail applications.

## Processing Recommendations

- **Gentle handling required:** Flakes are larger and more brittle than powder; minimize handling steps to reduce breakage.
- **Packaging:** Aluminum foil vacuum bags recommended for retail to preserve the deep red color consumers pay a premium for.
- **No further processing intended:** Special Grade flakes are a final-form product; not recommended as raw material for grinding (excessive cost premium).
