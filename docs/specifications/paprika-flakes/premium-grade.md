**Description:** Premium Grade paprika flakes are produced from fully ripened *Capsicum annuum* L. pods that are dried whole, then coarse-cut to a 4–8 mesh flake size (2.4–4.8 mm). The flakes retain bright red color (ASTA 160–200) and provide visual identity in finished products where recognizable paprika pieces are desired. The coarse cut ensures minimal fines, making them ideal for gourmet spice blends, meat rubs, and decorative garnish applications.

## Processing Recommendations

### Cutting & Sieving
Flakes are cut to 4–8 mesh (2.4–4.8 mm). Fines (through 20 mesh) are limited to ≤10% to maintain piece identity. For coarser cut (2–4 mesh, 4.8–9.5 mm), confirm availability with production scheduling.

### Blending Guidelines
- **With powders:** Add flake ingredients before powders to prevent powder settling to mixer bottom. Use gentle agitation — high-shear mixing breaks flakes and increases fines.
- **With other coarse ingredients:** Blends uniformly with dried garlic flakes, onion flakes, bay leaves, and coarse sea salt.
- **Storage after blending:** Keep blended flakes in sealed containers — the increased surface area (vs. whole pods) accelerates moisture pickup.

### Dosing Recommendations
- **Dry rubs:** 10–30% w/w
- **Spice blends:** 5–20% w/w
- **Garnish applications:** 1–5% w/w

## Storage Stability Data

| Condition | Shelf Life | Expected Outcome |
|-----------|------------|------------------|
| 15–20°C, ≤55% RH, dark | 18 months | ASTA loss <10% |
| 20–25°C, ≤60% RH, dark | 12 months | ASTA loss 10–15% |
| >30°C or >65% RH | Not recommended | Caking, color loss, mold risk |
| Sealed, unopened | 12–18 months | Per temperature |
| After opening | 4–6 months | Reseal immediately; store cool & dark |

**Critical:** Flakes have greater void space than powder — they attract moisture more readily in humid conditions. Always store in sealed packaging.
