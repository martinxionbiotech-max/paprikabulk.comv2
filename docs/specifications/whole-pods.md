# Whole Paprika Pods — Specification

## Product Identification

| **Product Information** | |
|-------------------------|---|
| Product Name | Whole Dried Paprika Pods |
| Product Code | WP-STD-001 |
| Grade | Standard (single-grade for whole pods) |
| Product Form | Whole dried pods (stem-on, as harvested) |
| Botanical Source | *Capsicum annuum* L. (various Xinjiang/Shandong cultivars) |
| Intended Market | Further processing, Middle East & Africa whole-pod trade |
| Regulatory Status | Food-grade, compliant with applicable import standards |
| HS Code | 0904.21.00 (dried, neither crushed nor ground) |
| Country of Origin | China (Xinjiang / Shandong / Gansu) |

**Description:** Whole dried paprika pods are sun-dried or hot-air-dried *Capsicum annuum* L. pods harvested at full red-ripe maturity. Retaining stems, seeds, and internal membranes, these pods serve as raw material for further processing (grinding into powder, cutting into flakes) or for direct sale into markets that prefer whole dried chili peppers. Color value (ASTA 140–200) indicates suitability for downstream grinding into Superior to Premium grade powders.

## Processing Recommendations

### Drying Method
- **Sun-dried pods** (Xinjiang, desert climate): Lower cost, natural process. May show wider color variation.
- **Hot-air dried** (Shandong, controlled tunnel drying): More consistent moisture and color uniformity.
- Specify preference at time of order. Bag labels note drying method.

### Sterilization Prior to Processing
Whole pods are NOT sterile by default. For food-safe grinding:

| Method | Typical Dosage | Effect on Quality | Cost Premium |
|--------|---------------|-------------------|--------------|
| Steam sterilization | 90–100°C, 10–15 min | Minimal color loss (<5%) | Moderate |
| Ethylene oxide (EO) | 500–800 ppm | No color loss; regulatory restricted in EU | Low |
| Irradiation | 5–10 kGy | Minimal change; labeling required in some markets | Moderate |
| NOT recommended | — | — | — |
| UV treatment | — | Surface only; ineffective on whole pods | — |

### Grinding Yield Estimation
- 1 kg whole pods ≈ 0.85–0.90 kg powder (stem and seed loss = 10–15%)
- Stem removal and deseeding before grinding improves color quality of finished powder
- Deseeding removes ~20–25% of weight but reduces SHU (most capsaicin resides in internal membranes and seeds)

### Dosing for Processing
- **Deseeded grinding:** 0.75–0.80 kg finished powder per kg whole pods
- **Non-deseeded grinding:** 0.85–0.90 kg finished powder per kg whole pods, with slightly higher SHU

## Storage Stability Data

| Condition | Shelf Life | Expected Outcome |
|-----------|------------|------------------|
| 15–25°C, ≤60% RH, dark | 18–24 months | Color stable; ASTA loss <10% |
| 25–30°C, ≤65% RH, dark | 12–18 months | ASTA loss 10–20% |
| >30°C or >65% RH | 6–12 months | Mold risk significant; insect infestation possible |
| Whole pods (unprocessed) | Generally 12–24 months | Longer than powder due to intact cell structure |

**Critical:** Whole pods retain moisture longer than powder. Verify moisture ≤12% at arrival — if higher (>14%), re-dry immediately before storage to prevent mold. Store off the floor on pallets. Rotate FIFO/FEFO.
