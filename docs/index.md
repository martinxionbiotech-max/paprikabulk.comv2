This comprehensive knowledge base covers every aspect of paprika sourcing, quality, and trade:

### Product Portfolio
- **Paprika Powder** — Premium (ASTA 160–200), Superior (ASTA 120–160), Standard (ASTA 80–120), Special (ASTA 200+)
- **Paprika Flakes** — All four grades in coarse-cut form for garnish and visible-piece applications
- **Whole Pods** — Premium-grade (ASTA 140–200) for on-site grinding or direct trade

### Quality Parameters
- **ASTA Color Standards** — ASTA 20.1 spectrophotometric methodology, ISO 7541 equivalence
- **CIELAB Color Measurement** — L*a*b* values, a*/b* ratio for hue assessment
- **Particle Size Specifications** — Sieve analysis protocols per ISO 2591-1 and ASTM E11
- **Moisture Content** — LOD at 105°C, GAB model monolayer values, a_w correlation
- **Microbiological Limits** — EU, US FDA, China GB, Japan standards per pathogen
- **Heavy Metals** — Pb, Cd, As, Hg limits across 6 major regulatory markets
- **Mycotoxin Monitoring** — Aflatoxin B1, total aflatoxins, ochratoxin A by HPLC-MS/MS
- **Pesticide Residue Management** — MRL compliance for EU (EC 396/2005), US (40 CFR 180), China (GB 2763), Japan (Positive List), Codex
- **Pungency Measurement** — Scoville Heat Units (SHU) by HPLC, capsaicinoid profiling
- **Capsanthin Content** — Dominant red carotenoid, quality marker, HPLC quantitation
- **Antioxidant Activity** — ORAC, DPPH, singlet oxygen quenching by carotenoids

### Food Safety & Regulatory Compliance
- **EU** — EC 1881/2006 (contaminants), EC 396/2005 (pesticides), ESA Quality Minima, EC 2073/2005 (microbiology)
- **United States** — FSMA Foreign Supplier Verification Program (FSVP), FDA 21 CFR, Preventive Controls for Human Food (PCHF), Food Traceability Rule (FSMA 204)
- **China** — GB 2762-2022 (contaminants), GB 2763 (pesticide MRLs), GB 4789 series (microbiology)
- **Japan** — Positive List System for pesticides, Food Sanitation Act limits
- **Codex Alimentarius** — CXS 193-1995 (contaminants), CXM 2-2023 (pesticide MRL)
- **Certifications** — HACCP, ISO 22000/9001, Kosher (OU/OK), Organic (EU/USDA/NOP), Gluten-Free

### Supply Chain & Procurement
- **Incoterms** — FOB, CIF, CFR, EXW with paprika-specific cost allocation
- **Container Loading** — 20 ft and 40 ft FCL loading configurations, desiccant placement
- **Origin Sourcing** — Xinjiang (China), Gansu (China), Hungary, Spain (La Vera PDO), Serbia, California, India
- **Harvest Seasons** — Peak harvest windows by origin, blending strategies
- **Supplier Qualification** — Questionnaires, audit checklists, scorecard evaluation
- **Quality Dispute Resolution** — Case studies, ASTA discrepancies, caking, border detention
