# Certificate of Analysis — Template

## 1. Purpose & Scope

This Certificate of Analysis (COA) template serves as the standard format for documenting the quality test results of every paprika batch shipped by Dinweys (Qingdao).Co.,Ltd. The COA accompanies each shipment and provides the customer with certified evidence that the product meets the agreed specification. Issuance of the COA is required prior to release for shipment.

**Scope:** All paprika powder, flake, and whole-pod products across all grades (Standard, Superior, Premium, Special).

## 6. Acceptance Criteria & Decision Rules

- **All parameters listed above** must fall within the stated specification range for the batch to pass.
- If any parameter is out-of-specification (OOS), the batch is placed **ON HOLD** and the QC Manager must initiate a deviation investigation (see [CAPA Report](../quality-control/capa-report.md)).
- A batch with critical non-conformance (Salmonella positive, heavy metal exceedance, or ASTA below minimum) is **REJECTED** and cannot be shipped as the declared grade.
- A batch with minor non-conformance (e.g., moisture 10.2% vs ≤10.0%) may be conditionally released upon written risk assessment and customer approval.

## 8. Recording & Data Retention

- The QC Analyst performing the tests must record all raw data in the Laboratory Information Management System (LIMS) or QC logbook.
- COA copies are retained for a minimum of **5 years** after the batch expiry date.
- Electronic COAs are stored in the Dinweys document management system with access controlled to QC and Sales departments.
- Customer-specific COA formats may be accommodated when specified in the purchase agreement.
